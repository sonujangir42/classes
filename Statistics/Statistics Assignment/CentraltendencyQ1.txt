Given: weekly sales data (in units)
Week 1: 50 units
Week 2: 60 units
Week 3: 55 units
Week 4: 70 units
1.Mean: M=(50+60+55+70)/4
         =>(235)/4
         =>58.75
2.Median: m=(55+60)/2
           =>(115)/2
           =>57.5
3.mode:The mode is the element that occurs most in the data set.
       In this case, all elements occur only once, so there is no mode.
